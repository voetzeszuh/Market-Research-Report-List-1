Type A connectors are commonly used for host devices and provide power and data transfer capabilities. Type B connectors are typically found in peripheral devices, facilitating communication with hosts. Type C connectors offer a more versatile design with a reversible connection, capable of high-speed data transfer and power delivery. This diversity allows for robust connectivity solutions in industrial applications.</p></p>
