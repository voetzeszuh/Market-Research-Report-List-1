<p><p>The automotive radar transceiver market primarily involves two key types: BiCMOS and RFCMOS. BiCMOS integrates both bipolar and CMOS technologies, offering high speed and low power consumption, making it suitable for complex signal processing in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). On the other hand, RFCMOS utilizes CMOS technology optimized for radio frequency applications, providing cost-effectiveness and efficiency for radar systems. Both technologies are essential in enhancing vehicle safety, enabling features like collision avoidance and adaptive cruise control.</p></p>
