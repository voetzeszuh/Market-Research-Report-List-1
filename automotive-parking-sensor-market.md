<p><strong>The Automotive Parking Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward</li><li>Rear View</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive parking sensor market is primarily segmented into forward and rear view types. Forward parking sensors are designed to assist drivers during low-speed maneuvers, detecting obstacles in front of the vehicle to prevent collisions while parking. In contrast, rear view parking sensors are mounted at the back to alert drivers of obstacles behind them, enhancing safety during reversing. Both types utilize ultrasonic or electromagnetic technology to provide audio or visual alerts, improving parking precision and driver confidence.</p></p>
