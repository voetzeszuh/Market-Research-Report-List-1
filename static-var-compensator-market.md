<p><strong>The Static Var Compensator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCR-based SVC</li><li>MCR-based SVC</li><li>TSC-based SVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Var Compensators (SVCs) are used in power systems to regulate voltage and enhance stability. TCR-based SVC uses Thyristor-Controlled Reactors to provide reactive power support, mainly improving voltage stability during peak loads. MCR-based SVC employs Mechanical Switching Capacitors, which offer a more robust solution for quicker reactive power support. TSC-based SVC utilizes Thyristor Switched Capacitors, enabling swift compensation for varying load conditions. Each type serves distinct roles in optimizing grid performance and managing power quality.</p></p>
