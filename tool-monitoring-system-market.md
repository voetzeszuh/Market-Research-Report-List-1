<p><p>The Tool Monitoring System market is divided into two primary types: Direct Monitoring System and Indirect Monitoring System. </p><p>Direct Monitoring Systems utilize sensors directly attached to the tools, providing real-time feedback on performance, wear, and condition. In contrast, Indirect Monitoring Systems gather data through external sources, such as machine parameters or environmental conditions, to infer tool status. Both systems aim to enhance operational efficiency, reduce downtime, and extend tool lifespan, catering to various industrial needs and preferences in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
