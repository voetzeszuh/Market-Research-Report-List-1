<p><p>The stormwater treatment equipment market comprises various types designed to manage and purify stormwater. Screening equipment removes debris and large particles, while separation equipment utilizes physical processes to isolate pollutants, such as oils and sediments. Filtration equipment further cleans water through diverse media, removing smaller contaminants. Infiltration equipment allows stormwater to percolate into the ground, enhancing groundwater recharge, while retention equipment captures and holds stormwater for gradual release or treatment. Other categories may include innovative technologies and methodologies.</p></p>
