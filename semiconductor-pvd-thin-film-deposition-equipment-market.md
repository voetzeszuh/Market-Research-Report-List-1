<p><p>The semiconductor PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) thin film deposition equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: vacuum evaporation and sputtering. Vacuum evaporation involves heating materials until they vaporize and then allowing the vapor to condense on the substrate, forming a thin film. Sputtering, on the other hand, utilizes ion bombardment to dislodge atoms from a target material, which then deposit onto the surface. Both methods are crucial for producing high-quality thin films in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
