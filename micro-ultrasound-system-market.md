<p><p>The micro ultrasound system market is characterized by two key types: non-invasive imaging and in-vivo imaging. Non-invasive imaging employs advanced ultrasound technology to capture high-resolution images without requiring surgical procedures, making it ideal for diagnostics and monitoring. In-vivo imaging, on the other hand, involves imaging directly within a living organism, providing real-time insights into biological structures and functions. Both types enhance clinical decision-making and improve patient outcomes by delivering critical information with minimal discomfort.</p></p>
