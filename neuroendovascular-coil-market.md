<p><p>The neuroendovascular coil market comprises two main types: bare metal coils and hydrogel-coated coils. Bare metal coils are made of pure metal and are primarily used for occluding cerebral aneurysms, providing a straightforward solution for vascular treatment. Hydrogel-coated coils feature a coating that expands upon contact with blood, promoting better tissue integration and supporting the healing process. Both types are essential for addressing neurovascular diseases, with hydrogel-coated coils often offering enhanced biocompatibility and performance in specific cases.</p></p>
