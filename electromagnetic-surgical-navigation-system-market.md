<p><p>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market is characterized by two primary types: Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic Navigation. Hybrid Navigation combines electromagnetic tracking with optical systems, offering enhanced accuracy and flexibility during surgeries, while also integrating various imaging modalities. In contrast, Electromagnetic Navigation relies solely on electromagnetic fields to track surgical instruments in real time, providing a less cumbersome setup and improved freedom of movement. Both types aim to enhance surgical precision, reduce errors, and improve patient outcomes during procedures.</p></p>
