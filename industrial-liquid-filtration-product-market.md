<p><p>The industrial liquid filtration product market encompasses various types designed for efficient separation of solids from liquids. **Filter presses** use plates to generate pressure for effective filtration, while **bag filters** capture particles through fabric bags, offering easy maintenance. **Drum filters** rotate to allow continuous filtration, making them suitable for high-volume processing. **Depth filters** utilize porous media to trap particles throughout the filter, enhancing retention. Lastly, **others** include innovative solutions like membrane filters, catering to specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
