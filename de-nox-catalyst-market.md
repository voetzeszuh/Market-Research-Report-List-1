<p><strong>The De-NOx Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Honeycomb</li><li>Flat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The De-NOx catalyst market primarily consists of two types: honeycomb and flat catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts, with their structured, channel-like design, enhance gas flow and increase catalytic efficiency, making them ideal for applications in automotive and industrial sectors. Flat catalysts, on the other hand, feature a simpler design and are often used in space-constrained environments. Both types effectively reduce nitrogen oxides emissions, but their choice depends on specific application requirements, space availability, and efficiency needs.</p></p>
