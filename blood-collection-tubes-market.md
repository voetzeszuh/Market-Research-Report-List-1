<p><p>The blood collection tubes market serves critical applications in both venous and capillary blood collection. Venous blood collection utilizes larger tubes for obtaining blood samples from veins, essential for comprehensive lab testing and diagnostics. Both methods are vital for patient care, facilitating accurate diagnoses, monitoring health, and guiding treatment decisions across various healthcare settings.</p></p>
