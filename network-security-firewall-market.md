<p><p>The network security firewall market comprises various types, notably SMS firewalls and signaling firewalls. SMS firewalls are designed to regulate and protect SMS traffic, safeguarding against fraud, spam, and unauthorized access while ensuring reliable messaging services. Signaling firewalls, on the other hand, focus on securing signaling protocols, particularly in mobile networks, to prevent threats like denial-of-service attacks and protocol misuse. Together, these firewalls enhance network integrity, user safety, and ensure compliance with standards in telecommunications.</p></p>
