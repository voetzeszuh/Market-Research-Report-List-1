<p><strong>The Phase Contrast Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Phase-Contrast Microscopy</li><li>Quantitative Phase-Contrast Microscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phase contrast microscopy market is categorized into two main types: traditional phase-contrast microscopy and quantitative phase-contrast microscopy. Traditional phase-contrast microscopy enhances the visibility of transparent specimens by converting phase shifts in light passed through the sample into variations in brightness. In contrast, quantitative phase-contrast microscopy provides a more detailed analysis by measuring the phase shift quantitatively, allowing for the reconstruction of the sample's refractive index distribution. This advancement enables more precise measurements in biological and material sciences.</p></p>
