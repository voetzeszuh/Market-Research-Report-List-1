<p><p>The Multiple Myeloma treatment market comprises several therapeutic types. Proteasome inhibitors target cellular degradation pathways to kill myeloma cells. Immunomodulatory agents (IMiDs) enhance the body's immune response against cancer. Histone deacetylase (HDAC) inhibitors interfere with cell cycle regulation and promote apoptosis. Immunotherapy stimulates the immune system to recognize and attack cancer cells. Lastly, cytotoxic chemotherapy employs drugs that kill rapidly dividing cells, effectively reducing tumor burden. Each type plays a distinct role in managing Multiple Myeloma.</p></p>
