Hang-on Foam Fractionators attach externally to the tank, providing flexibility and ease of maintenance. Sump Based Foam Fractionators are positioned in a sump, allowing for larger capacity and enhanced performance, making them ideal for larger aquariums or reef systems. Each type serves specific tank setups and user preferences.</p></p>
