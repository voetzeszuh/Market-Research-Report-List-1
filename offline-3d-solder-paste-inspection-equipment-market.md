<p><ul><li>Laser-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li><li>PMP-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Offline 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment market is primarily divided into two types: laser-based and PMP-based inspection equipment. Laser-based systems utilize laser triangulation to measure solder paste height and volume with high precision, making them suitable for complex PCB designs. In contrast, PMP (Photonic Measurement Principle)-based equipment employs advanced imaging techniques to assess the quality and consistency of solder paste deposits. Both types aim to enhance manufacturing accuracy and reduce defects in soldering processes.</p></p>
