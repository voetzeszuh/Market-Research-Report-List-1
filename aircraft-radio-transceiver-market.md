<p><p>The aircraft radio transceiver market includes several types, primarily categorized into VHF (Very High Frequency) and UHF (Ultra High Frequency) systems, along with other variants. VHF transceivers, operating between 30 MHz and 300 MHz, are widely used for air-to-ground communications due to their long-range capabilities. UHF transceivers, ranging from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, are often utilized in military applications for secure communications. Other types include satellite communication and digital systems, catering to specialized needs in aviation communication.</p></p>
