<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoionization Detector (PID)</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor (MOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld VOC detector market features three main types: Photoionization Detectors (PIDs), which utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds for detection; Flame Ionization Detectors (FIDs), which employ a flame to ionize gases, providing high sensitivity for hydrocarbons; and Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensors (MOS), which use a change in conductivity to detect VOCs, offering quick response times and compact designs. Each type serves various applications, from environmental monitoring to industrial safety, catering to diverse detection needs.</p></p>
