<p><p>The Water Mist Fire Fighting System market is categorized into open and closed nozzles. Open nozzles disperse water mist freely into the atmosphere, allowing for effective cooling and fire suppression over a larger area. In contrast, closed nozzles create a confined mist cloud, targeting specific fire zones and minimizing water usage. Both types enhance fire safety by reducing heat and smoke while being efficient and environmentally friendly, making them suitable for various applications, including industrial, marine, and residential settings.</p></p>
