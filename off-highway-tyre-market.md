<p><p>The Off Highway Tyre market is segmented into three main types: Bias Type, Radial Type, and Solid Type. Bias Type tyres have overlapping layers for flexibility and load distribution, suitable for rough terrains. Radial Type tyres feature a belt of steel cords allowing for better fuel efficiency and improved traction; they are ideal for longer distances. Solid Type tyres are puncture-resistant and designed for use on hard surfaces, providing durability and low maintenance, commonly used in industrial applications.</p></p>
