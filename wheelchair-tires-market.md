<p><p>The wheelchair tires market comprises three main types: air-filled, solid, and foam-filled tires. Air-filled tires offer superior shock absorption and a comfortable ride, making them suitable for varied terrains. Solid tires, while puncture-proof and low maintenance, may provide a harsher ride due to limited cushioning. Foam-filled tires combine the benefits of air-filled and solid options, offering a balance of comfort and durability without the risk of punctures. Each type caters to specific user needs and preferences in mobility and stability.</p></p>
