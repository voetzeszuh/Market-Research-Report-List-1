<p><ul><li>Near field Antenna Measurement System</li><li>Far-field Antenna Measurement System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Antenna Measurement System market is divided into two primary types: Near-field and Far-field measurement systems. Near-field systems assess antennas based on electromagnetic fields at close distances, providing high-resolution data for complex antenna designs. In contrast, Far-field systems evaluate antennas at greater distances, where the radiation pattern stabilizes, making them ideal for standard compliance and performance analysis. Both types are essential for optimizing antenna performance across various applications, including telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive industries.</p></p>
