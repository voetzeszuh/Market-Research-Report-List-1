<p><p>The ceramic packaging substrate material market primarily includes three types: Alumina substrate material, Aluminum Nitride (AlN) substrate material, and Silicon Nitride substrate material. Alumina substrates are favored for their excellent electrical insulation properties and cost-effectiveness. AlN substrates are known for superior thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-power applications. Silicon nitride substrates offer high mechanical strength and thermal stability, suitable for demanding environments. Each type caters to specific industry needs in electronics, automotive, and telecommunications.</p></p>
