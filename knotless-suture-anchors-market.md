<p><strong>The Knotless Suture Anchors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knotless suture anchors market is divided into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable. Absorbable anchors are designed to break down in the body over time, reducing the need for a second surgery and minimizing long-term complications. Non-absorbable anchors, on the other hand, provide permanent support and stability for repaired tissues, making them suitable for chronic or high-stress conditions. Both types cater to varying surgical needs, influencing their selection based on factors like patient recovery and the nature of injuries.</p></p>
