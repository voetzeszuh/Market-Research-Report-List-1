<p><p>The Ion Nitriding Equipment market is categorized into two primary types: Hot-Wall Ion Nitriding Equipment and Cold-Wall Ion Nitriding Equipment. Hot-Wall systems utilize heated walls to enhance ion activation and promote better nitriding uniformity, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, Cold-Wall systems maintain a lower temperature, reducing thermal effects on the components while allowing for finer control over nitriding depth and properties. Both types serve distinct applications based on material requirements and production processes.</p></p>
