<p><p>The Wrist Orthosis Market includes two primary types: Ordinary Splint Type and Inflatable Type. The Ordinary Splint Type features rigid structures designed to immobilize the wrist and provide support, commonly used for fractures or chronic conditions. In contrast, the Inflatable Type utilizes an adjustable air bladder system, allowing for customizable compression and support, enhancing comfort and mobility during rehabilitation. Both types aim to alleviate pain and promote recovery, catering to diverse patient needs in orthopedic care.</p></p>
