<p><strong>The HVDC (High-Voltage Direct Current) Converter Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCC</li><li>VSC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC converter transformers market is primarily divided into two types: Line Commutated Converter (LCC) and Voltage Source Converter (VSC). LCC technology is well-established, using thyristors and suitable for long-distance, high-power transmission, offering high efficiency but requiring synchronous grids. In contrast, VSC technology utilizes IGBTs, providing flexibility for integrating renewable energy sources, allowing for compact designs and independent control of active and reactive power. Both types serve critical roles in modern power systems, enhancing grid reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
