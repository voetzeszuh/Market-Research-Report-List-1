<p><p>The toilet sensor flushometer market is primarily categorized into dual-flush and single-flush types. Dual-flush systems offer two flush options—one for liquid waste and another for solid waste—promoting water conservation by allowing users to select the appropriate flush volume. In contrast, single-flush systems provide a uniform flush volume for all waste types, simplifying operation but potentially using more water. Both types address efficiency in water usage and are pivotal in modern restroom designs, catering to sustainability and user convenience.</p></p>
