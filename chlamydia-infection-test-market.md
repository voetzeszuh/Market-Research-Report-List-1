<p><p>The Chlamydia infection test market primarily includes Nucleic Acid Amplification Tests (NAATs) and Direct Fluorescent Tests. NAATs are highly sensitive and specific, detecting Chlamydia DNA in various samples, making them the preferred choice for accurate diagnosis. In contrast, Direct Fluorescent Tests rely on fluorescently labeled antibodies to identify the presence of the bacteria in samples, offering rapid results but with lower sensitivity. Both testing types are crucial for effective screening and management of Chlamydia infections in clinical practice.</p></p>
