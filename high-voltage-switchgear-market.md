<p><p>The high-voltage switchgear market comprises two main types: Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) and Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS). GIS utilizes gas as an insulator, allowing for compact designs suitable for space-constrained areas, minimizing maintenance and offering high reliability. In contrast, AIS relies on air for insulation, making it more cost-effective and easier to maintain in open environments. Both technologies serve crucial roles in electrical grids, facilitating the safe and efficient distribution of electricity in various applications.</p></p>
