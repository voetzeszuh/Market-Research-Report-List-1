<p><p>The EV On Board Battery Charger market is characterized by two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional chargers. Unidirectional chargers facilitate energy transfer solely from the grid to the electric vehicle, optimizing charging efficiency. In contrast, bidirectional chargers allow energy flow in both directions, enabling electric vehicles to return stored energy to the grid or power homes. This enhances grid stability, supports energy trading, and maximizes the utility of renewable energy sources, making bidirectional chargers increasingly popular in the evolving EV landscape.</p></p>
