<p><p>The nanoparticle measuring devices market comprises several technologies, primarily Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS) and Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis (NTA). DLS measures the size distribution of nanoparticles by analyzing scattered light fluctuations, providing rapid size assessments in suspension. In contrast, NTA visualizes individual nanoparticles using microscopy and tracks their movement, delivering detailed size and concentration information. Both methods are essential in applications such as drug formulation, environmental monitoring, and materials science, enabling precise characterization of nanoparticles.</p></p>
