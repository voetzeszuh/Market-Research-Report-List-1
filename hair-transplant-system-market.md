<p><strong>The Hair Transplant System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Strip Surgery (FUSS)</li><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair transplant system market primarily includes three techniques: Follicular Unit Strip Surgery (FUSS), Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT), and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUSS involves removing a strip of scalp skin containing hair follicles, which are then transplanted. FUT, often used interchangeably with FUSS, focuses specifically on the transplantation of individual follicular units. FUE is less invasive, extracting individual follicles directly from the scalp without a strip. Each method has unique advantages, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
