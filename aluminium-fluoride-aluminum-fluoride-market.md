<p><p>The aluminum fluoride market comprises three main types: dry AlF3, anhydrous AlF3, and wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 is typically used in aluminum smelting processes and exhibits high purity. Anhydrous AlF3, which lacks water content, is preferred for specific chemical applications and contributes to enhanced efficiency in aluminum production. Wet AlF3 contains water, is often less expensive, and is commonly utilized in industries like ceramics and glass. Each type serves distinct applications, impacting overall demand in the market.</p></p>
