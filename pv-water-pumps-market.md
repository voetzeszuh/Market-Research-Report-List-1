<p><p>The PV water pumps market primarily consists of surface suction and submersible types. Surface suction pumps are installed above the water source, using suction to draw water into the system; they are ideal for shallow applications. In contrast, submersible pumps operate underwater, pushing water to the surface, making them suitable for deeper sources. Each type caters to specific needs in agricultural, residential, and industrial sectors, enhancing water accessibility and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
