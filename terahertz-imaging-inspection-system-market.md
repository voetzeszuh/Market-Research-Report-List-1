<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection System market is divided into two main types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging utilizes naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, allowing for non-invasive inspections without the need for external sources. In contrast, active terahertz imaging employs external terahertz sources to illuminate the subject, enhancing image clarity and detail. Both methods are crucial in applications such as security screening, materials characterization, and biomedical imaging, each providing unique advantages based on the inspection requirements.</p></p>
