<p><p>Grid Connected PV Systems involve the integration of solar panels, power conditioning units, grid connection equipment, and inverters. Grid connection equipment ensures safe and reliable interfacing with the power grid. Inverters play a crucial role by converting direct current (DC) generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC), enabling the electricity to be used or fed back into the grid.</p></p>
