<p><p>The large format advertising digital inkjet printing equipment market comprises three main types: water-based inkjet, solvent inkjet, and UV inkjet. Water-based inkjet printers use eco-friendly inks, suitable for indoor applications and providing vibrant colors. Solvent inkjet printers utilize solvent-based inks, ideal for outdoor use due to their durability and resistance to weather elements. UV inkjet printers use ultraviolet light to cure inks instantly, allowing for versatile printing on various substrates, including rigid materials, making them popular for both indoor and outdoor applications.</p></p>
