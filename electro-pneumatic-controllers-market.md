<p><p>Electro-pneumatic controllers are essential in industrial automation, converting electrical signals into proportional pneumatic outputs. The market is divided into two main types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current). DC controllers offer more precise control and are favored in applications requiring quick response times and energy efficiency. In contrast, AC controllers are often used in larger systems where robust power handling is necessary. Both types serve diverse industries by enhancing process control, reliability, and efficiency in automation systems.</p></p>
