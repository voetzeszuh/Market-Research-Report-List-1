<p><p>The Steel Wire Tire Cord market is classified into various types based on tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cord is used for standard tires, offering basic performance. High Tensile (HT) cord provides enhanced strength and durability, suitable for high-performance tires. Super Tensile (ST) cord offers even greater strength and flexibility, often used in heavy-duty applications. Ultra Tensile (UT) cord delivers the highest level of strength and resistance, ideal for extreme conditions and specialized vehicles, enhancing tire longevity and safety.</p></p>
