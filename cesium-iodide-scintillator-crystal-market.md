<p><p>The Cesium Iodide Scintillator Crystal market comprises three main types: Cesium Iodide (Pure), Cesium Iodide (Tl), and Cesium Iodide (Na). Cesium Iodide (Pure) offers high light yield and excellent energy resolution, making it suitable for various applications. Cesium Iodide (Tl) is doped with thallium, enhancing its scintillation properties, which is favored in medical imaging and radiation detection. Cesium Iodide (Na) incorporates sodium, providing a different scintillation response and often used in specialized applications, including gamma spectroscopy.</p></p>
