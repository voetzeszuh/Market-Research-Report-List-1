<p><p>Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs) are emergency signaling devices designed to help locate individuals in distress. The market is primarily segmented into two types based on frequency: 406MHz locator beacons and 121.5MHz locator beacons. The 406MHz beacons are more advanced, offering satellite-based tracking and higher accuracy, making them suitable for search and rescue operations. In contrast, 121.5MHz beacons, while older and less accurate, still provide basic broadcasting capabilities for emergency situations, primarily relying on ground-based monitoring. Both types are essential for personal safety in remote areas.</p></p>
