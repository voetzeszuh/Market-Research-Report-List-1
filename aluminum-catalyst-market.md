<p><p>The aluminum catalyst market is segmented into activated and non-activated types. Activated aluminum catalysts enhance reaction rates by promoting chemical reactions through increased surface area or reactivity, often employed in processes like hydrogenation and polymerization. Non-activated aluminum catalysts, on the other hand, do not require such modifications and are typically used in more straightforward catalytic applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, with activated catalysts being favored for complex reactions requiring higher efficiency, while non-activated catalysts are suitable for simpler processes.</p></p>
