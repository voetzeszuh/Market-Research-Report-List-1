<p><strong>The CT X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT X-ray tube market comprises two main types: stationary anode and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed target for X-ray production, suitable for low-power applications but limited in heat capacity. In contrast, rotating anode tubes have a rotating target that allows for higher heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-demand clinical environments. Rotating anodes enhance image quality and longevity of the equipment, driving their preference in advanced medical imaging applications.</p></p>
