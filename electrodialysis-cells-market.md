<p><p>The electrodialysis cells market comprises two main types: continuous electrodialysis and batch electrodialysis. Continuous electrodialysis operates in a constant flow, allowing for ongoing separation of ions in a stream of solution, making it efficient for large-scale applications. In contrast, batch electrodialysis processes are conducted in discrete batches, providing flexibility and control over the treatment process, which is suitable for smaller volumes or specific ion removal tasks. Both types serve critical roles in water desalination, wastewater treatment, and food processing.</p></p>
